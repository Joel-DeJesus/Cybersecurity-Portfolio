Small Business Security Audit

Scenario · Framework: CIS Controls · Time: 3–4 hours

Scope

Assessed a 25-employee environment covering asset management, access control, patching, and backups.

Method

  1. Interviewed stakeholders; reviewed existing policies and architecture.
  2. Mapped gaps against CIS Controls v8.
  3. Produced a prioritized remediation roadmap with effort/impact ratings.

Key Findings
