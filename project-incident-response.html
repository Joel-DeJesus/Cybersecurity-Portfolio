Phishing Incident Response

Scenario · Tools: SIEM, EDR, Email Gateway · Time: 2–3 hours

Objective

Investigate and contain a simulated phishing email with a malicious link sent to multiple users.

Process

  1. Queried SIEM for related events (sender, subject, URL, recipients).
  2. Enriched IOCs with threat intel; verified domain reputation.
  3. Identified impacted accounts; initiated password resets & session revocation.
  4. Quarantined emails & blocked sender at the gateway.
  5. Documented the timeline in the Incident Handler’s Journal.

Outcome

  • Containment within 2 hours; no confirmed exfiltration.
  • Users notified and trained on reporting spoofed senders.
  • Added detection rule for similar patterns.
